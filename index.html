about us

How It All Started?

When you want to escape from the metropolitan crowd in the center of the city, when you need to store energy with a short break, or when you have the most exciting organizations on your special days that make your face smile more differently than ever, we start each new day with the happiness and responsibility of being the first address that comes to your mind for 21 years.


The biggest motivation of both our creative teams in the kitchen and our service team, which always tries to offer you the best, is to succeed in becoming a brand that produces the latest ingredients that always direct the Sunday we are in in our branch.


GAEGY Café, which is the favorite brand of everyone from seven to seventy, has also been awarded the most visited Cafe award by Boğaziçi University students 3 times. As the GAEGY Cafe team, we are very pleased to welcome you to our esteemed guests at our Etiler branch between 09:00-01:00 on Dec 7 days a week; at our Kartal branch between 10:00-22:00 on Dec 7 days a week, on our magnificent garden/viewing terrace and peaceful view. In order to experience more together, we are waiting for our nearest branch as soon as possible… Because “We are happy with you!”.
